Interviewer: How do you feel about our pricing?

Participant: For our small team of eight, the starter plan is affordable. We pay less than we did for the old tool.

Interviewer: Any concerns?

Participant: When we looked at the enterprise tier for SSO and audit logs, the jump felt steep. Our finance lead said it was almost double what competitors quoted for similar features.

Interviewer: Would you upgrade?

Participant: Maybe next year if we grow past fifty seats. Right now the mid-tier is fine, but missing advanced permissions is annoying.

Interviewer: Compared to competitors?

Participant: Notion and spreadsheets are cheaper but manual. Your automation saves us time, so I can justify the cost to my manager—for small teams at least.

Interviewer: Anything else?

Participant: Transparent pricing page would help. I had to book a sales call to understand seat minimums on enterprise.
